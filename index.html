How it works

Layered AI control —
your rules, every level

The same AI feature — say, the AI Tutor — behaves differently across your institution based on the governance layer that applies. Each level inherits from above and can only narrow, not broaden, the permitted behaviour.

Organisation LevelSet by: IT Admin / DPO
"Always respond in formal academic language. Never provide direct answers to graded assessment questions. Only reference approved course materials. Do not engage with topics outside the current course syllabus."
Inherited ↓ narrowed by
Department LevelSet by: Head of Department
"Additionally: restrict responses to the domain of medical science. Reference CBME competency framework when explaining clinical topics. Use the European Pharmacopoeia as the standard reference for pharmacology questions."
Inherited ↓ narrowed by
Course LevelSet by: Faculty Instructor
"Additionally: focus explanations on Unit 3 content only. Encourage students to attempt problems before requesting explanations. Respond in German when the student writes in German."
